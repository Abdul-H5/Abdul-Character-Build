Lindsay Bluth Fünke

Lindsay Bluth Fünke is the adoptive daughter of George and Lucille Bluth. She is selfish, materialistic, and vain, constantly seeking attention and approval. Despite her flaws, she occasionally shows moments of compassion, particularly for her daughter Maeby.

x

Buster Bluth

Byron "Buster" Bluth is the youngest Bluth son, known for his extreme dependence on his mother, Lucille. He is socially awkward, prone to panic attacks, and has a hook for a hand after a run-in with a "loose seal." Buster is often childlike and easily manipulated by his family.
